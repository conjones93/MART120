~ About K.B. ~


I have been supporting myself as a stained glass artist for the last 24 years. For many years I lived on, and drew inspiration from, Vashon Island, Washington. My studio is currently located in Missoula, Montana.

I draw my inspiration from the natural world, all that is seen and unseen. Spirit guides my hands and eyes, my heart, by the mystery and beauty of the earth.

It is my hope and intent, that my images inspire a moment of peace which is a gift for me, the beholder, and the world.
